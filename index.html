The process for Testosterone Replacement Therapy is simple. You will complete a brief medical intake form, pay $95 for initial lab work and consultation, and then receive your treatment if prescribed. Lab work is completed at LabCorp or Quest Diagnostics. We offer several TRT options, including oral, gel, and injectable forms of testosterone. The $95 payment covers the initial lab work and consultation, and is applied to your first month of treatment. The cost of treatment varies depending on the option you choose:

  • Oral: $104/first month, then $199/month

Side effects of testosterone replacement therapy can include:

  • Acne
  • Shrinkage of testicles
  • Reduced sperm count
  • Testicular atrophy

Your doctor will monitor your progress and adjust your dosage as needed. If you experience any side effects, please contact your doctor immediately.
